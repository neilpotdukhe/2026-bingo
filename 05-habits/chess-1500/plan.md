# Chess 1500 Elo

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Reach 1500 Elo rating (Chess.com or Lichess)
**Deadline:** End of 2026

## Why This Matters
Mental sharpness. Pattern recognition. Lifelong skill.

## Current Level
~1100 Elo

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Reach 1500 rated (rapid or blitz)
- [ ] Maintain for 20+ games

## Plan
1. **Daily practice (30 min):**
   - 10 min tactics (Chess.com puzzles or Lichess)
   - 20 min games (rapid preferred for learning)
2. **Weekly study:**
   - Review own games (use engine analysis)
   - Learn 1-2 openings deeply (as white and black)
   - Study basic endgames
3. **Focus areas for 1100 ’ 1500:**
   - Stop blundering pieces (slow down, check for threats)
   - Basic tactics: forks, pins, skewers
   - Opening principles (develop, castle, control center)
   - Simple endgames (K+Q, K+R, K+P)
