## Why This Matters
Endurance challenge. Meditative experience. See a place differently on foot.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Walk 15+ continuous miles in one day
- [ ] GPS or app verified distance

## Plan
1. Pick a route:
   - Urban exploration (walk across a city)
   - Trail/nature walk
   - Point A to B with purpose
2. Build up with progressively longer walks
3. Prepare: good shoes, water, snacks, podcasts/music
4. Pick good weather day
5. Consider making it social or solo
