# Shenzhen, China Trip

**Status:** Planning
**Target:** Build supplier relationships for USA hardware business + explore BCI/neurotech partnerships
**Deadline:** TBD

## Why This Matters
1. **BCI/Neurotech:** Find hardware partners for neural interface products (consumer wellness positioning)
2. **General Hardware:** Establish supplier relationships for US import business
3. **Market Intel:** See what's trending 6-12 months before it hits USA

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Spend 5-7 days in Shenzhen (+ optional Shanghai/Guangzhou)
- [ ] Visit Huaqiangbei electronics markets (SEG, Huaqiang Electronics World)
- [ ] Tour 3+ factories/suppliers (EEG/sensors, general electronics, packaging)
- [ ] Meet with at least 1 BCI company (NeuroXess, Neuracle, or BrainCo)
- [ ] Return with 5+ vetted supplier contacts on WeChat
- [ ] Collect samples for 2-3 product categories
- [ ] Understand MOQs, lead times, and landed costs for target products

## Business Strategy

### Stream 1: Quick Cash (Fund the venture)
- **Product:** Repair parts OR trending accessories
- **Model:** Amazon FBA + direct to repair shops
- **Target:** $5-10K/month profit in 6 months
- **Trip goal:** Find 2-3 reliable suppliers, negotiate pricing, get samples

### Stream 2: Brand Play (The big swing)
- **Product:** Consumer EEG headband for focus/meditation
- **Model:** Hardware from Shenzhen + your software/app + US brand
- **Positioning:** Wellness (no FDA needed)
- **Target:** Launch 12-18 months, $20K+/month by month 24
- **Trip goal:** Visit EEG OEMs, understand capabilities, get dev samples

### Stream 3: B2B Relationships (Compound over time)
- **Service:** Sourcing/QC for US neurotech and robotics startups
- **Model:** 15-25% markup or consulting fees
- **Trip goal:** Build factory relationships, document capabilities

## Regulatory Notes (USA Import)

### Tariffs (as of 2025-26)
| Category | Tariff Rate | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Consumer electronics | 25% | Section 301 |
| Semiconductors | 50%+ | Increasing 2027 |
| Accessories/parts | 7.5-25% | Sweet spot |
| Medical devices | Variable | Exclusions available |
| Batteries (Li-ion) | Increasing | Watch this |

- 178 exclusions extended to Nov 2026 - check if products qualify
- Apply for exclusions at ustr.gov

### Compliance Requirements
| Requirement | Products | Cost | Timeline |
|-------------|----------|------|----------|
| FCC | Anything with radio (WiFi, BT) | $3-10K | 4-8 weeks |
| UL | AC-powered devices | $5-15K | 6-12 weeks |
| FDA 510(k) | Medical devices | $50K+ | 6-24 months |
| None | Consumer wellness (no medical claims) | $0 | Immediate |

**Key insight:** Consumer EEG = unregulated if positioned as wellness only
