# Make Pav Bhaji

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Cook authentic pav bhaji from scratch
**Deadline:** 

## Why This Matters
Classic Mumbai street food. Comfort food. Cooking skill.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Make bhaji from scratch (not from mix)
- [ ] Toast pav properly with butter
- [ ] Tastes like the real thing

## Plan
1. Find authentic recipe (Ranveer Brar or Tarla Dalal)
2. Get ingredients:
   - Pav bhaji masala
   - Potatoes, cauliflower, peas, tomatoes, onions
   - Butter (lots)
   - Pav buns
3. Make bhaji - mash vegetables properly
4. Toast pav on griddle with butter
5. Garnish: onion, cilantro, lemon
