# E2E Chicken (End-to-End)

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Process a chicken from live animal to cooked meal
**Deadline:** 

## Why This Matters
Understand where food comes from. Intentional eating. Humbling experience.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Participate in or perform humane slaughter
- [ ] Process/clean the bird
- [ ] Cook and eat

## Plan
1. Find opportunity:
   - Local farm that offers processing experience
   - Hunting preserve
   - Friend with chickens
2. Learn humane slaughter techniques beforehand
3. Learn processing steps: plucking, gutting, cleaning
4. Choose preparation: roast, braise, etc.
5. Cook with intention and gratitude
