# Build Pizza Oven at Novi House

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Build a functional outdoor pizza oven
**Deadline:** Summer 2026

## Why This Matters
Gathering spot. Great pizzas. Satisfying build project.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Oven reaches 700-900°F
- [ ] Can cook pizza in 60-90 seconds
- [ ] Looks good in the backyard

## Plan
1. Research oven types:
   - Kit (easiest): Ooni, Gozney, prefab dome
   - DIY brick/cob oven (more work, more character)
2. Choose location at Novi house
3. Plan foundation/base
4. Order materials in spring
5. Build in early summer
6. Test and dial in cooking
