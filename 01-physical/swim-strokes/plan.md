# Swim All Strokes Competently

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Be competent at freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly
**Deadline:** Before scuba certification

## Why This Matters
Water confidence. Foundation for scuba. Complete swimmer.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Freestyle: 100m continuous, proper breathing
- [ ] Backstroke: 50m continuous, straight line
- [ ] Breaststroke: 50m continuous, proper timing
- [ ] Butterfly: 25m continuous, legal stroke

## Plan
1. Find adult swim lessons (local pool or private coach)
2. Start with weakest stroke
3. Weekly lessons + 1-2 practice sessions
4. Master one stroke before moving to next
5. Order: Freestyle ’ Backstroke ’ Breaststroke ’ Butterfly
