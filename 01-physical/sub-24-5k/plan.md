# Sub 24 Minute 5K

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Run 5K in under 24:00
**Deadline:** 

## Why This Matters
Solid running fitness benchmark. Requires consistent training discipline.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Official or GPS-verified 5K under 24:00
- [ ] Pace: under 7:44/mile

## Plan
1. Establish current 5K time (baseline test)
2. Build weekly mileage (3-4 runs/week)
3. Mix easy runs, tempo runs, and intervals
4. Weekly structure:
   - 2x easy runs (conversational pace)
   - 1x tempo run (goal pace)
   - 1x intervals (faster than goal pace)
5. Race or time trial monthly to track progress
