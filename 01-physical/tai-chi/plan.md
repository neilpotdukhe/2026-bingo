# Tai Chi Basics

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Learn fundamental Tai Chi movements and one short form
**Deadline:** 

## Why This Matters
Mind-body practice. Good for mobility, balance, and stress. Morning routine potential.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Know basic stances and weight shifts
- [ ] Can perform one short form (8-10 movements) from memory
- [ ] Practice feels natural and flowing

## Plan
1. Find local class or online instruction
2. Start with basics: stance, weight shift, arm movements
3. Learn a beginner form (Yang style 8-form is common)
4. Practice 10-15 min daily
5. Focus on slow, intentional movement
