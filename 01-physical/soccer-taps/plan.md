# 20 Soccer Ball Taps in a Row

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Juggle soccer ball 20 times consecutively (any body part)
**Deadline:** 

## Why This Matters
Basic ball control skill. Quick daily practice habit.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 20 consecutive taps without ball touching ground

## Plan
1. Start with dominant foot only
2. Practice 10-15 min daily
3. Progression:
   - 5 taps ’ 10 taps ’ 15 taps ’ 20 taps
4. Once consistent with foot, add thigh touches
5. Film attempts to track progress
