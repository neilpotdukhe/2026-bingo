# Dunk Basketball

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Dunk a regulation basketball on a 10ft rim
**Deadline:** 

## Why This Matters
Iconic athletic achievement. Requires explosive power and coordination.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Dunk regulation ball on 10ft rim
- [ ] Clean dunk (not just tip-in)

## Plan
1. Measure current standing reach and vertical
2. Calculate inches needed to dunk
3. Start plyometric program (box jumps, depth jumps, jump squats)
4. Strengthen legs (squats, deadlifts, lunges)
5. Practice approach and timing with lower rim
6. Progressive rim height increase
