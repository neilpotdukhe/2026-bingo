# Backflip

**Status:** Not Started
**Target:** Land a standing backflip on flat ground
**Deadline:** 

## Why This Matters
Ultimate athletic confidence move. Proves body control and conquers fear.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Land backflip unassisted on flat ground
- [ ] Consistent enough to do on demand

## Plan
1. Find a gym with foam pit or spring floor
2. Get coaching (gymnastics or tricking coach)
3. Build comfort with backward rotation (back rolls, back handsprings)
4. Progress: trampoline ’ foam pit ’ spring floor ’ flat ground
5. Drill until consistent
